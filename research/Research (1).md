### **If you're a local SMB (single/multi-location)**

1.  **Max out your GBP**: categories, services, menu/services schema,
    > hours, photos, Q&A, products, updates. This is the #1 feed for
    > Maps and a major eligibility signal for AI Overviews. [[Google
    > Help+1\
    > ]{.underline}](https://support.google.com/business/answer/2721884?hl=en-419&utm_source=chatgpt.com)

2.  **Mirror to Bing Places** (and keep it in sync): import from GBP,
    > verify, and maintain NAP, categories, and photos. Copilot uses it;
    > ChatGPT often piggybacks on Bing data. [[Whitespark\
    > ]{.underline}](https://whitespark.ca/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-your-bing-places-listing/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

3.  **Reviews where engines look**: Google, Bing-connected networks
    > (Yelp/Facebook historically inform Bing), and verticals
    > (Tripadvisor for hospitality). [[Search Engine Journal+1\
    > ]{.underline}](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/microsoft-bing-local-search-results-now-powered-by-yelp/44954/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

4.  **On-site LocalBusiness schema** + location pages: helps all engines
    > understand service areas, hours, and offerings. (Bing explicitly
    > encourages structured data.) [[Search - Microsoft Bing\
    > ]{.underline}](https://www.bing.com/webmasters/help/webmasters-guidelines-30fba23a?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

5.  **Be citation-worthy**: publish concise, factual pages (pricing,
    > policies, FAQs, comparisons) that engines can quote and link to in
    > AI answers. Perplexity, in particular, rewards clear evidence.
    > [[Ehukai Media\
    > ]{.underline}](https://ehukaimedia.com/blog/perplexity-citations-for-local-businesses/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

### **If you're an online/virtual business**

1.  **Design pages to be cited**: authoritative explainers, feature
    > matrices, transparent pricing, docs---clean headings and fact
    > nuggets that LLMs can lift with attribution (Perplexity/ChatGPT).
    > [[Seabuck+1\
    > ]{.underline}](https://seabuckdigital.com/perplexity-ai-search-engine-features/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

2.  **Product/Organization schema** (and FAQ/HowTo where valid):
    > increases the chance AI Overviews and Copilot summarize you
    > correctly. [[Search - Microsoft Bing\
    > ]{.underline}](https://www.bing.com/webmasters/help/webmasters-guidelines-30fba23a?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

3.  **Brand authority & partnerships**: ChatGPT Search references
    > partner content providers; strong coverage in reputable
    > publications can echo into AI citations.
    > [[Reuters]{.underline}](https://www.reuters.com/technology/artificial-intelligence/openai-adds-new-search-function-chatgpt-2024-10-31/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
